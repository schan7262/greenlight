Classroom Activities

Everyday, toddlers get involved in fun and educational classroom activities like these!

Sensory Bins Music & Movement Puzzle Play
Fill containers with materials like rice, sand, or water beads, and let toddlers explore these textures. Play fun songs and encourage toddlers to dance, clap, or play simple instruments like tambourines or maracas. Provide age-appropriate puzzles with large, easy-to-grasp pieces. Puzzles help with problem-solving, spatial awareness, and hand-eye coordination.
